Delay-differential equation · Reed instrument model

Barjau, Agulló & Cardona 1997 (PDF)

Model definition

Two coupled DDEs for pressure $p(t)$ and reed velocity $u(t)$ with discrete delay $T$ and polynomial nonlinearity $f_{nl}$.

$$\frac{du}{dt} = \frac{f_{nl}(p) - u}{\tau}$$

$$\frac{dp}{dt} = \frac{Z_0}{S}\!\left[\frac{f_{nl}(p)-u}{\tau} + \sigma\, u(t) - \beta\, u(t-T)\right] - \sigma\, p(t) - \beta\, p(t-T)$$

$$f_{nl}(p) = \alpha\,(P_0 - p)\,(p - p_f)$$
